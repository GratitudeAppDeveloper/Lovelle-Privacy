Privacy Policy for Future Me

Effective date: 29/04/2026

Lovelle respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Lovelle collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may access or process:

  • Location data, to provide local weather information in the Day section.
  • Subscription and purchase status, to determine access to premium themes and membership features.
  • App usage and device-related information, as provided by Apple or app services for functionality, security, and performance.

How We Use Information

We use information only to operate and improve the app, including to:

  • show local weather relevant to your device location,
  • unlock and manage premium membership access,
  • maintain app functionality and performance, and
  • support security, troubleshooting, and reliability.

Location Information

If you allow location access, Future Me may use your device’s current location to retrieve weather information for the Day tab. If you deny location access, weather-related features may be limited or unavailable.

Subscriptions and Payments

Premium memberships and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not directly collect or store your full payment card information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may rely on Apple services or trusted service providers only as necessary to operate app features such as subscriptions, weather, and platform functionality.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Children’s Privacy

Lovelle is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
